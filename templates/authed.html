plug.dj playlist uploader
This site allows you to upload your plug.dj playlists to your Youtube account.
1. Drag this javascript bookmarklet to your bookmark bar.
2. Navigate to plug.dj, log in and select the playlist that you wish to upload to Youtube. Click on the bookmarklet and a list of Youtube IDs will appear at the bottom of the page underneath the footer.
3. Login to your youtube account
4. Paste the list of IDs from plug.dj into the large box, type a name for your playlist in the small box (it must be a new playlist). Click Submit!
5. Bask in the glory of being to listen to your plug.dj playlist anywhere and everywhere!
